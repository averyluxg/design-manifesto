Time to replace the empty rhetoric of “building a better world”

Tech is not above us. It should be governed by all of us.

Progress is more than innovation. Let us create a new Renaissance.

Let us build from trust. We need digital citizens, not mere consumers.

Design open to scrutiny. We must question our intent and listen to our hearts.
